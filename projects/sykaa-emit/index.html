The problem

In 2022, Sykaa began a project to build an entirely new platform from the ground up to serve as the most sophisticated project for the world to experience a new beginning of an AR World. The platform framing is currently in progress, and several members are working together to build this web-based platform. The primary goals for the project are reducing barriers and making the platform easier for new users, but with plenty of room to scale for advanced users.

The Sykaa design system

To streamline the design process across designers and engineers for such a sophisticated project, it is important to lay the foundations with a strong, a flexible design system that could evolve during the product’s development cycle. This would inform both the aesthetics and user experience across the product itself as well as the website and marketing material.

Gesture control system

A gesture system is highly important for the working of this project, so we put together a comprehensive documentation website to cover and train about principles, management, accessibility, and component guidelines for designers and engineers working with the system.

Motion capture

Animation and gesture controls are core principle in making the authoring experience a more fluid process. Elements animate in ways that indicate the cause and effect of each interaction to improve the fluidity of the overall experience.

Encouraging adaptivity

A major part of solving for collaboration is being able to visualize the user experince before deployment. This is especially beneficial for subject matter experts and users who need to review and give feedback on the higher level structure without having to dig through all of the adaptivity scenarios part by part.

An extensible plugin ecosystem usable by everyone

The most powerful aspect of the platform is the ability to create custom plugins for any content, whether it be a model, gesture, device, surface, or interactive component. Out of the box, these can be made configurable with minimal effort from developers. Designers can then edit everything using a common configuration interface.

Next-generation revolution experiences

The flexibility of the project will allow developers to create engaging interactive experiences as highly configurable plugins that could then be used and manipulated by normal clients.

Bring out graphics alive

Designers and Developers can make any graphics and media content come alive and then configure camera positions to animate to for each section as per gesture control.

Interactivity

People can then be directed to specific parts of the model and shown labels with their gestures. They’re also able to click and drag to orbit around and freely explore their alive virtual world.

Animation

Developers and Designers can pick an animation included in the model to play or loop for any section without having to use any complex interface.

Project outcomes

Ultimately the project build process have been successfully initiated by our team members to become an eye opening revolution for next generation world.
